CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor essential for hepatocyte growth,
CC       the differentiation of plasma cells, the immunoglobulin secretion,
CC       and the unfolded protein response (UPR). Acts during endoplasmic
CC       reticulum stress (ER) by activating unfolded protein response
CC       (UPR) target genes via direct binding to the UPR element (UPRE).
CC       Binds DNA preferably to the CRE-like element 5'-
CC       GATGACGTG[TG]N(3)[AT]T-3', and also to some TPA response elements
CC       (TRE). Binds to the HLA DR-alpha promoter. Binds to the Tax-
CC       responsive element (TRE) of HTLV-I.
