CC   -!- FUNCTION: Carbohydrate-binding lectin with a preference for
CC       chitin. May play a role in defense against pathogens, or in tissue
CC       remodeling. May play an important role in the capacity of cells to
CC       respond to and cope with changes in their environment.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Monomer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted, extracellular space.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Present in activated macrophages, articular
CC       chondrocytes, synovial cells as well as in liver. Undetectable in
CC       muscle tissues, lung, pancreas, mononuclear cells, or fibroblasts.
CC   -!- PTM: Glycosylated.
CC   -!- DISEASE: A genetic variation in CHI3L1 is associated with
CC       susceptibility to asthma-related traits type 7 (ASRT7)
CC       [MIM:611960]. Asthma-related traits include clinical symptoms of
CC       asthma, such as coughing, wheezing and dyspnea, bronchial
CC       hyperresponsiveness (BHR) as assessed by methacholine challenge
CC       test, serum IgE levels, atopy, and atopic dermatitis.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the glycosyl hydrolase 18 family.
