CC   -!- DISEASE: Defects in ACSL4 are the cause of mental retardation X-
CC       linked type 63 (MRX63) [MIM:300387]. Mental retardation is a
CC       mental disorder characterized by significantly sub-average general
CC       intellectual functioning associated with impairments in adaptative
CC       behavior and manifested during the developmental period. Non-
CC       syndromic mental retardation patients do not manifest other
CC       clinical signs.
CC   -!- DISEASE: Defects in ACSL4 are involved in Alport syndrome with

CC       [MIM:300194]. A X-linked contiguous gene deletion syndrome
CC       characterized by glomerulonephritis, deafness, mental retardation,
CC       midface hypoplasia and elliptocytosis.
