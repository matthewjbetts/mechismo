CC   -!- FUNCTION: Seems to play a role in testicular function. May be a
CC       trophic hormone with a role in testicular descent in fetal life.
CC       Is a ligand for LGR8 receptor.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Heterodimer of a B chain and an A chain linked by two
CC       disulfide bonds.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in prenatal and postnatal Leydig
CC       cells. Found as well in the corpus luteum, trophoblast, fetal
CC       membranes and breast.
CC   -!- DISEASE: Defects in INSL3 seems to be a cause of cryptorchidism
CC       (CRYPTO) [MIM:219050]; also known as impaired testicular descent.
CC       It is one of the most frequent congenital abnormalities in humans,
CC       involving 2-5% of male births. Cryptorchidism is associated with
CC       increased risk of infertility and testicular cancer. The frequency
CC       of INSL3 gene mutations as a cause of cryptorchidism is low.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the insulin family.
