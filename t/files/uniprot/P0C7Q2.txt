CC   -!- DISEASE: Defects in ARMS2 influence susceptibility to age-related
CC       macular degeneration type 8 (ARMD8) [MIM:613778]. ARMD is the most
CC       common cause of irreversible vision loss in the developed world.
CC       In most patients, the disease is manifest as ophthalmoscopically
CC       visible yellowish accumulations of protein and lipid (known as
CC       drusen) that lie beneath the retinal pigment epithelium and within
CC       an elastin-containing structure known as Bruch's membrane. ARMD is
CC       likely to be a mechanistically heterogeneous group of disorders,
CC       and the specific disease mechanisms that underlie the vast
CC       majority of cases are currently unknown. However, a number of
CC       studies have suggested that both genetic and environmental factors
CC       are likely to play a role.
